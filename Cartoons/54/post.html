Like a dream

Dreams suck. I was prone to nightmares as a kid, which gave me particular umbrage when reading Roald Dahl's The BGF, as I wondered what that big oaf's problem was and why he'd keep blowing nightmares into my bedroom with his stupid trumpet.

On reflection, my only theory was that he kept aiming for my brother and was a terrible shot.
