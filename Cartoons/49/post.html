Antonioh-no

For a silly pun for my simple tastes, this one had a surprising number of challenges to it.

The first was how to express the speech in such a way that it could be humerous whether you knew the language or did not.

The second was the comic's name. It was originally the same as the punchline, but I found that seeing it upfront diluted the delivery of the joke entirely. I still wanted it to link to Captain Corelli in some way, just without giving the game away.
