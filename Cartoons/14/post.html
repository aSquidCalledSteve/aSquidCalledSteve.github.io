Shaolin Plumber

You might recognise this toon from the article On giving and receiving compliments. This was originally penned to just act as a supporting toon but... some silliness just cannot be contained.

I sat on this a while, labouring over whether duplicating a toon like this would be tacky. Then I thought "Dude... they're your toons. Shut up."

On a more serious note, editing this was pretty harrowing as an old lady's balcony overlooks my office, so I was perpetually worried that she'd see in and wonder why I had a giant backside splashed across my screen for a few hours.
