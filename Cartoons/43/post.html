Odin's finest 2

Valheim

All based on true events.

Bunk beds seem like a great space-saving idea until they cause you to defy the laws of physics in what becomes your straw-laden (albeit comfortable) prison. In fact, beds in general are a dangerous entity to misplace.

Imagine, if you will, returning home to your lodgings after a successful day's hunt only to hear a cry in your headset "Guys, I'm stuck in the roof" and look over to see the following.

This comic was born entirely from the final panel. It was a concept sketch for the previous comic which, I confess, tickled me endlessly to the point that I just had to find a home for it.
